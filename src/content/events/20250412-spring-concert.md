Join us for our annual Spring concert, "A Celebration of the Saxophone." This evening showcases the incredible versatility of our instrument, with performances from both our Ensemble and the full Saxophone Orchestra.

Under the baton of Caroline Fernandes, the program includes a world premiere of a new commission by our patron Andy Scott, alongside transcriptions of orchestral classics and high-octane jazz arrangements.

## Event Programme

Our carefully curated programme spans several centuries of music, highlighting the unique sound world of the saxophone choir.

- **Andy Scott:** *New Commission (World Premiere)*
- **J.S. Bach:** *Brandenburg Concerto No. 3 (arr. for Saxophone Orchestra)*
- **Astor Piazzolla:** *Libertango*
- **George Gershwin:** *An American in Paris*

Tickets are available via the Stag Theatre box office or at the door.
